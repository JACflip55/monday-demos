# Angel Investor Quick Decision Framework

*A one-page guide for evaluating early-stage deals*

## The 5-Minute Quick Take

Before diving deep, answer these four questions:

| Question | 🟢 Proceed | 🔴 Pass |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **Do I believe in this founder?** | Yes, I'd work with them | No chemistry/trust |
| **Is this a real problem?** | I've seen this pain | Seems invented |
| **Can I add value?** | My network/expertise helps | Just writing a check |
| **Does the math work?** | Reasonable terms | Valuation is crazy |

If you can't answer "yes" to at least 3 of 4, pass now. Don't waste time on deep diligence.

## The Angel Scoring Framework

Rate each factor 1-10, then weight:

### 1. Founder Quality (30%)
Angels bet on people. Ask:
- [ ] Domain expertise: Do they *really* know this space?
- [ ] Commitment: Full-time? Invested own money?
- [ ] Coachability: Will they listen when they're wrong?
- [ ] Track record: Have they built/accomplished things before?
- [ ] Network: Can they hire, sell, and raise?

**Score: ___/10**

### 2. Traction (25%)
Proof beats projections. Look for:
- [ ] Active users or paying customers
- [ ] Revenue (any amount shows willingness to pay)
- [ ] Growth trajectory (month-over-month)
- [ ] Engagement/retention (do people come back?)
- [ ] Social proof (testimonials, pilots, LOIs)

**Score: ___/10**

### 3. Market Opportunity (20%)
Realistic, not fantasy. Consider:
- [ ] Problem severity: Is this a vitamin or painkiller?
- [ ] Willingness to pay: Are there budgets for this?
- [ ] Competition: Crowded or differentiated?
- [ ] Timing: Why is now the right moment?
- [ ] Access: Can THIS team reach customers?

**Score: ___/10**

### 4. Capital Efficiency (15%)
How far will the money go?
- [ ] Runway: How many months at current burn?
- [ ] Burn rate: Is spending sensible?
- [ ] Milestones: What will this money achieve?
- [ ] Previous use: Did they use last round well?
- [ ] Amount fit: Is the raise appropriate for stage?

**Score: ___/10**

### 5. Exit Potential (10%)
Be realistic about outcomes:
- [ ] Acquirer interest: Who might buy this?
- [ ] Strategic value: Tech or team worth acquiring?
- [ ] Revenue path: Can this become profitable?
- [ ] Timeline: 5-7 year horizon realistic?
- [ ] Comparables: Similar companies that exited?

**Score: ___/10**

## Valuation Sanity Check

| Stage | Typical Range (2026) |
|-------|---------------------|
| Pre-seed | $1M - $5M cap |
| Seed | $3M - $15M cap |
| Series A | $10M - $40M pre |

**Red flags:**
- Pre-revenue company at $10M+ cap
- No traction at seed valuations
- "We'll figure out monetization later"

## Questions to Always Ask

1. **Why you?** What makes you uniquely qualified?
2. **Why now?** What changed to make this possible?
3. **What's your unfair advantage?** (Not "we work hard")
4. **What keeps you up at night?** (Self-awareness test)
5. **What happens if this doesn't work?** (Plan B reality check)
6. **How do I get my money back?** (Exit awareness)

---

## Pro Tips

- **30 minutes max** for initial evaluation
- **Trust your gut** on founder quality
- **Pass fast** — your time is your scarcest resource
- **Add value or don't invest** — the best deals want smart money
- **Diversify** — expect 60-70% failure rate
- **Check sizes: 1-2% of portfolio** per deal
