📚 Why Portfolio Math Matters

📊 The Power Law: ~65% of angel investments return less than 1x. But the top 1-2% return 30-100x+. Your portfolio must survive enough failures to catch a winner.
🎯 Diversification is insurance: With 10 deals, there's a ~10% chance you miss a 10x+ entirely. With 30 deals, that drops to ~1%. More shots on goal = more chances to catch the power law tail.
💰 Follow-on math: Reserving 25-33% for follow-ons into winners can boost returns significantly — but only if you have signal on which companies are actually winning (not just "feels good").
⚖️ The tradeoff: Concentrated portfolios have higher variance (bigger wins OR bigger losses). Diversified portfolios have lower variance but may cap upside through smaller check sizes that limit pro-rata access.
🏆 The research: Kauffman Foundation data shows angel portfolios of 15-20+ deals significantly outperform concentrated ones. The "spray and pray" label is misleading — it's really "spray and survive long enough to win."
